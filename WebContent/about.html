About Club

Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington, London, England, that plays in the Premier League, the top flight of
English football. The club has won 13 League titles, a record 13 FA Cups, 2 League Cups, 15 FA Community Shields,
1 League Centenary Trophy, 1 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup and 1 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup.

THE ARSENAL FOUNDATION

The Club’s grant-giving organisation aims to transform the lives of young people via a range of education and sport projects in the UK and overseas.
The Arsenal Foundation uses the power of football and the Arsenal name to inspire and support young people in north London and across the globe.
Just knowing Arsenal cares can be a huge source of motivation for individuals facing difficulties in their lives.

ARSENAL IN THE COMMUNITY

Since launching in the 1980s, Arsenal in the Community has helped individuals and local communities to thrive and become the best they can be.
Arsenal in the Community has been using the power of the Arsenal name to positively impact the lives of young people since 1985. The team delivers
sport, social and education programmes to over 5,000 individuals each week
